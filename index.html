About Universitas Indonesia Supermileage Vehicle Team

UI Supermileage Vehicle Team is a group of students who represent Universitas Indonesia in Kontes Mobil Hemat Energi (KMHE) and Shell Eco-Marathon Asia (SEM Asia). Two teams - Nakoela (Prototype Gasoline) and Sadewa (Urban Gasoline) send annually to the competitions.

Since 2010, UI-SMV has succeed to constantly won national or international trophies each year. Last year, despite all the limitation due to COVID-19 breakout, UI-SMV still managed to win six categories from Kontes Mobil Hemat Energi (KMHE). Adjusting to a full-online competition, this year, Sadewa's team from UI-SMV won 2nd place beating more than 50 foreign teams as well as being the only Indonesian representative in the Shell Eco-Marathon, Road to 2050.

With numerous challenges from Shell Eco Marathon and KMHE coming ahead, we will need supports to win and make our university and Indonesia proud.

We Design

Since 2010, we have focused on researching and creating our best design to invent the most fuel-efficient vehicles.

We Build

Our workshop is located at Fakultas Teknik Universitas Indonesia. We work in our workshop daily to build our cars, test our researches, or test our cars and continue working on our researches. We always do our best to ensure the safety of our members and follow the health protocols carefully.

We Race

We will secure our titles and prepare the most efficient vehicle for KMHE 2021 and SEM Asia 2022.
